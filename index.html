My Leave Summary

Joining date: —

PeriodEntitlement
1. Annual Leave

1.1 10 days per full calendar year from the following year.

1.2 First year: Jan–Mar = 14d, Apr–Jun = 10d, Jul–Sep = 7d, Oct+ = 4d.

1.3 No Annual Leave during Training or Probation.

1.5 Apply at least 07 days in advance.

2. Casual Leave

2.1 07 days per calendar year.

2.2 First year: ½ day per completed month (max 6 days / 12 half-days).

2.3 From next calendar year: full 7 days from 1 January.

3. Duty Leave

For official company activities. Requires Superior and HOD approval.

4. Lieu Leave

≥8 hrs on holiday = 1 day lieu; 4½–8 hrs = ½ day. Use within same/next month.

5. Maternity Leave

84 working days with pay. 14 pre + 70 post confinement. Excludes weekends and Poya.

6. Leave to Vote

Paid leave of at least 4 hours for Parliamentary, Presidential, or Provincial elections.

7. No Pay / Unauthorized Absence

Unauthorized absence = no pay + disciplinary action. Authorized No Pay at Board's discretion.

8. Extension of Leave

Only in exceptional circumstances. Communicate before leave expires.

9. Overstay of Leave

3 consecutive days absent without intimation = deemed to have vacated post.

10. Quarantine Leave

For PHI/medical officer ordered quarantine. Covid-positive: use sick leave instead.
